Test Structure (2025 Update)

The ACT is now often delivered digitally in the U.S. Core sections: English, Math, Reading, Science (Writing is optional). Total time: 2 h 55 m (plus breaks).

Sections & Timing

  • English: 45 min, 75 questions
  • Math: 60 min, 60 questions
  • Reading: 35 min, 40 questions
  • Science: 35 min, 40 questions
  • Writing (optional): 40 min, 1 essay

How to Study

Take full-length, timed practice tests; review every missed question. Supplement with drills and quizzes from official sources and free resources below.

Recent Changes to the ACT

In 2025, ACT shifted to a shorter, digital-friendly format delivered in both online and paper formats. Content remains the same, but the experience now includes in-test timers, highlighting tools, and streamlined interface—check your test center’s format on MyACT before your exam.
