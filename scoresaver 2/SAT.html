What the SAT Is (2025 Digital Format)

The SAT is fully digital now. It takes about 2 hours and 14 minutes and has two parts: Reading and Writing and Math. The test is adaptive. Your second module gets easier or harder based on how you do on the first one. UPDATE: Some of the links have been taken down by their organizer. Sorry!

Sections and Timing

  • Reading and Writing: 64 minutes, 54 questions
  • Math: 70 minutes, 44 questions

How to Study

Start with an official full-length practice test. Review every mistake and write down what to fix. Study in short blocks, a few times a week. Use digital tools so the practice feels like the real thing.

Why It Matters

Many schools are test optional, but a strong SAT score can help with admissions and scholarships. It also shows readiness across different schools and classes.
