Test Structure (Digital SAT 2025)

The SAT is now fully digital. It takes about 2 hours and 14 minutes. The old test had 3 sections, but the digital SAT only has two: Reading & Writing and Math. The new test uses adaptive testing, meaning questions change based on your performance.

Sections and Timing

  • Reading & Writing: 64 minutes (2 modules, 54 questions total)
  • Math: 70 minutes (2 modules, 44 questions total)

How to Study

Use full-length practice tests and prep tools from College Board and Khan Academy. Try to simulate test-day conditions: timed, no distractions. Review mistakes and master concepts.

Why the SAT is Important

Even though many colleges are test-optional, a high SAT score can improve your application and help you earn scholarships. For competitive schools, a strong score still matters.
